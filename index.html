About

I am a researcher at Meituan Inc., where I work on large multimodal models, diffusion models, and AI design agents.

My research is particularly focused on building commercial image generation systems — spanning the full stack from data curation, training of large-scale generative models, to rigorous evaluation of generation quality.

Previously, my work mainly explored the application of multimodal large language models to fine-grained visual perception — including language-guided segmentation, multi-granular visual understanding, and self-reflection frameworks for multimodal understanding and generation.
